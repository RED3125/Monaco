MONACO

The Principality of Monaco is a dwarf state on the coast of the Ligurian Sea, receiving almost a million tourists a year. Despite its more than modest size – the country's area fits into a ridiculous 2 km2 – Monaco looks incredibly respectable and glossy. Ports full to overflowing with snow-white yachts, a bunch of serious banks that attract money from all over Europe, and a well-established mechanism of tourist infrastructure help the local population to live in a big way, not denying themselves anything.

  • AREA - 2 km2
  • POPULATION - 38 350 persons
  • LANGUAGE - Français
